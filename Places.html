Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona, Spain is one of the best travel locations. It maybe very touristy but it is incaputered by lots of good architecture. It is best to go at the beginning of the summer because it is less crowded. Make sure before going to Spain exchange your money in the the airport because after you exit the airport the exchange is a lot more expensive. In Barcelona you will find the Barcelona Olympic Statium, La Rambla, lots of churches, and musuems. Finally the food is the best because there are lots of place to eat including choices from seafood, italian, and all american.

Naples/Pompeii, Italy

Naples/Pompeii is one of the vastest places in Italy. To see everything you want to see you will need about a week or two. This is because you will want about 2 days to do the lost city of Pompeii, 1 day to tour around Mount Vesuvius, and the other days to do everything you want to in Naples. They mostly have Italian and Seafood to eat there but it is very delicous.

Rome, Italy

This was my favorite place to travel in Italy. This is because there was an enourmous amount of things to do there. From seeing the Trevi Fountain, to seeing all of the hills that Rome was built on, to just walking around the city and seeing old archieture. Don't forget also that they have the Roman Colosseum. The city has so many side streets full of places to eat, which is mostly Italian food. Hope you enjoy.
